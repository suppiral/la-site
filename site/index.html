Little Adventure is a retro Action-Adventure indie game based on 90's SNES game like Legend of Zelda and Secret of Mana. it that tells the story of a girl that helps the people that lives in the small town she lives in with their problems, even if sometimes those problems leads to fighting monsters and retrieving items from dungeons.

The game was made as a final project in OOP2 class 2013 in Hadassah College by Gil Mizrahi.
